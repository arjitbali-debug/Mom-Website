Systemic practice

Constellation Work

Facilitated exploration of family, organizational and trauma-related patterns within a systemic framework.

01

Family Constellations

Explores recurring patterns, loyalties, exclusions, roles and relationships within a family system. Dr. Bali's training includes work associated with the Bert Hellinger Institute.

02

Business & Organizational Constellations

Applies systemic inquiry to organizations, leadership, teams, succession, family businesses and decision-making contexts.

03

Trauma Constellations

Approaches constellation work with greater attention to pacing, boundaries, resourcing and the risks of overwhelm or re-enactment.

What a session may involve

A concern or question is clarified, relevant elements of the system are identified, and representatives or markers may be positioned to make relational patterns visible. The facilitator supports observation and exploration rather than prescribing a single interpretation.

Scope and boundaries

Constellation work is experiential and should not be represented as a scientifically validated diagnostic method. It is not a replacement for psychotherapy, psychiatric care, legal advice, organizational consulting or trauma treatment by an appropriately licensed professional.

Breathwork

Breathwork and Rebirthing

Dr. Bali has also trained as a breathwork teacher through the Sadgamaya Foundation under Mahesh, according to information provided by the family. Breath practices are adapted to the participant and should be used cautiously when significant medical or psychiatric conditions are present.
