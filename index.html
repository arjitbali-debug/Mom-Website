Professional foundation

Special Educator before therapist

Dr. Bali began her career in special education at the B.M. Institute of Mental Health in Ahmedabad and subsequently worked in schools in Mumbai, Gurgaon and Kanpur. She later served as Principal of Samarpan, Centre for Autism Spectrum Disorders, established by SOPAN.
