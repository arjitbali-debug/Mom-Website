What is Biodynamic Craniosacral Therapy?

Biodynamic Craniosacral Therapy is a gentle hands-on approach that works with the body's natural rhythms and supports regulation of the nervous system. Sessions are intended to support relaxation and wellbeing and complement—not replace—appropriate medical care.
