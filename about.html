Biography

Dr. Meeta Bali

Special Educator, Biodynamic Craniosacral Practitioner, constellation facilitator, breathwork teacher, researcher and author.

Professional biography

Dr. Meeta Bali has worked in special education for more than three decades. Public profiles describe the beginning of her career at the B.M. Institute of Mental Health in Ahmedabad, followed by work as a special educator in Mumbai, Gurgaon and Kanpur.

She later served as Principal of Samarpan, Centre for Autism Spectrum Disorders, a project of SOPAN—the Society of Parents of Children with Autistic Disorders. Her educational and research interests include communication, visual strategies, autism, neurodevelopment and individualized learning support.

Her later professional development expanded into Biodynamic Craniosacral Therapy, systemic constellation work, HANDLE, breathwork and related body-based approaches.
