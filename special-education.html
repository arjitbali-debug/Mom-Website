Primary professional field

Special Education

Individualized, respectful support for learning, communication and development—with particular experience in Autism Spectrum Disorder.

Education that begins with the individual

Dr. Bali's longest-standing professional work is in special education. Her approach considers communication, sensory processing, movement, cognition, relationships, environment and family context rather than relying on a single standardized pathway.

She has worked with children and families in educational institutions and private practice and has held school leadership responsibility as Principal of Samarpan.

Samarpan and SOPAN

Leadership in autism education

SOPAN

SOPAN is the Society of Parents of Children with Autistic Disorders, a registered NGO working in autism and related developmental disabilities. It began in 2002 as an initiative of parents seeking education and training for their children.

Samarpan

Samarpan was established by SOPAN in February 2003 as a centre for children with autism. Dr. Bali served as its Principal.

Research-informed practice

Communication through visual strategies

In a 2007 study co-authored with Dr. Rubina Lal, 30 children with autism participated in research on visual strategies using objects, pictures, symbols and manual signs. The study reported significant improvement in communication skills for the intervention group.
